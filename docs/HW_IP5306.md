# HW_IP5306

Tags: #done #hardware #pmic

Power Management IC for battery charging and 5V boost. Driver: [[FW_BSP_IP5306]].

---

## Peripheral Overview

- **I2C Address**: `0x75`.
- **Function**: Battery charging, battery level reading, 5V power supply.

## Register Map

| Register | Address | Function |
|----------|---------|----------|
| SYS_CTL0 | 0x00    | Power on/off, boost enable |
| SYS_CTL1 | 0x01    | Charging voltage (4.1V/4.2V) |
| READ0    | 0x70    | Battery level / Status |

## Critical Constraints

### Auto Power-Off
**Must be disabled** in `SYS_CTL1` to prevent the system from shutting down during low-current standby (e.g., when audio is idle).

### Charging Voltage
Configured at **4.1V** for battery longevity.
